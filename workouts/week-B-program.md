# Week B — Lower Body + Core
**Cycle**: B of 4 | **Est. Time**: 35–40 min
**Focus**: Quads · Glutes · Hamstrings · Calves · Core

> Rest 60–90 seconds between sets.

---

## Block 1 — Legs (22–25 min)

### 1. Goblet Squat `3 × 15`
- **Target muscles**: Quads, glutes, core
- **Clint**: 50–60 lbs KB/DB | **Wife**: 20–30 lbs
- Wide stance, toes out. Chest tall throughout.

### 2. Romanian Deadlift (RDL) `3 × 12–15`
- **Target muscles**: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back
- **Clint**: 80–100 lbs total (2 DBs) | **Wife**: 30–40 lbs total
- Hinge at hips. Feel the hamstring stretch. Slow eccentric.

### 3. Bulgarian Split Squat `3 × 10–12 each leg`
- **Target muscles**: Quads, glutes, hip flexors
- **Clint**: 30–40 lbs total | **Wife**: 10–20 lbs total
- Rear foot on bench. Control descent. Great for bouldering footwork stability.

### 4. Glute Bridge / Hip Thrust `3 × 15`
- **Target muscles**: Glutes, hamstrings
- **Clint**: 45–70 lb DB across hips | **Wife**: 20–35 lb DB
- Full hip extension at top. Hold 1 second.

### 5. Calf Raises (Standing) `3 × 20–25`
- **Clint**: bodyweight + 30 lbs | **Wife**: bodyweight
- Full range of motion. Slow down = more effective.

---

## Block 2 — Core (12–15 min)

### 6. Hanging Knee Raise `3 × 12–15`
- Controlled movement. No swinging.

### 7. Side Plank `3 × 35 sec each side`
- Full body in one line. Can add hip dip for extra work.

### 8. Ab Rollout (from knees) `3 × 10–12`
- Keep lower back from sagging. Extend as far as stable.

### 9. Bicycle Crunch `3 × 20 each side`
- Slow and deliberate. Elbow toward opposite knee.

### 10. L-Sit Hold (dip bars or parallel bars) `3 × 15–20 sec`
- Compression tension. Excellent climbing carryover.

---

## Notes
- Bulgarian split squats are single-leg work — mirrors the precise weight distribution in bouldering.
- RDL is a crucial hamstring exercise often neglected by people who walk/run a lot.
- Hip thrusts are the highest-value glute exercise — don't skip them.
- If gym is crowded: swap Bulgarian Split Squat → Walking Lunges.
