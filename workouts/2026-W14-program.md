# Week A — Upper Push + Core
**Week of**: April 6, 2026 | **Cycle**: A of 4 | **Est. Time**: 35–40 min
**Focus**: Chest · Shoulders · Triceps · Core

> No warm-up or cool-down included. You handle that at the gym.
> Rest 60–90 seconds between sets. Keep weight challenging by rep 13–15.

---

## Block 1 — Push (20–22 min)

### 1. Dumbbell Bench Press `3 × 12–15`
- **Target muscles**: Chest, front delts, triceps
- **Clint**: ~70–80 lbs total | **Wife**: ~20–30 lbs total
- Feet flat, neutral spine. 3-second eccentric.

### 2. Incline Dumbbell Press `3 × 12–15`
- **Target muscles**: Upper chest, front delts
- **Clint**: ~60–70 lbs total | **Wife**: ~15–25 lbs total
- 30–45° incline. Control descent.

### 3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press (Standing) `3 × 12–15`
- **Target muscles**: Deltoids, triceps
- **Clint**: ~40–50 lbs total | **Wife**: ~15–20 lbs total
- Standing engages core throughout.

### 4. Lateral Raises `3 × 15`
- **Target muscles**: Medial deltoids
- **Clint**: 20–25 lbs | **Wife**: 5–8 lbs
- Light weight, lead with elbows. Slight forward lean.

### 5. Tricep Pushdown (Cable) `3 × 15`
- **Target muscles**: Triceps
- **Clint**: 40–50 lbs | **Wife**: 15–20 lbs
- Elbows locked at sides. Full extension.

---

## Block 2 — Core (12–15 min)

### 6. Dead Bug `3 × 10 each side`
- Slow and controlled. Lower back flush to floor throughout.

### 7. Pallof Press `3 × 12 each side`
- **Clint**: 30–40 lbs | **Wife**: 10–15 lbs
- Stand sideways to cable. Anti-rotation challenge.

### 8. RKC Plank `3 × 30–45 sec`
- Squeeze glutes, pull elbows toward toes. Much harder than standard plank.

### 9. Cable Woodchop (High to Low) `3 × 12 each side`
- **Clint**: 30–40 lbs | **Wife**: 10–15 lbs
- Rotate from torso. Control the return.

### 10. Hollow Body Hold `3 × 25 sec`
- Arms overhead, legs low. Lower back pressed to floor.

---

## Notes
- Push exercises are bouldering antagonists — they balance the heavy pulling/crimping load from climbing.
- The shoulder press standing version fires more core stabilizers — good yoga/climbing carryover.
- If gym is crowded: swap Cable Woodchop → Russian Twist with plate; swap Pallof Press → Side Plank.
